About Us

A Legacy of Clear Vision
& Compassionate Care

Founded in 2005, Sridhar Netralaya has grown into one of Karnataka's most trusted eye care institutions, blending world-class technology with a patient-first philosophy.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest ophthalmic diagnostic and surgical equipment. From routine eye exams to complex microsurgeries, our team of highly trained ophthalmologists delivers exceptional outcomes with the utmost care and precision.
